Muslim Companion

Muslim Companion is an Islamic Mobile Application for Android, iOS and Progressive Web Application. It contains Holy Quran Audio and Translation in about 90 Languages. It also has Prayer Times, Azan, Qibla Finder, Hadiths and many more..

app features

Quran Translation

User can read Holy Quran in 80 different languages with Juzz and Ayah filter and many more

Quran Audio

Quran Audio Recitation is also available with selection of different great reciter around the World

Prayer Time

Five Times Daily Prayer (Salat) Time is available based on User Geolocation and Country time

Hadiths

Complete Collection of Hadiths, Chapters and Details are also available for the users to read

Qibla Finder

User can find qibla direction when they want to perform Salat using the Qibla finder features

Community

Community is for users to post, comment and interact on Islamic Subjects and provide advice

Hijra Calendar

The Hijra Calendar correspondence to Gregorian Calendar for user to check Date and Islamic Events

Weather

User Can check weather condition based on Geolocation and time of each Countries around the World

Many more..

And many more features such as Names of Allah, Tasbah, Near by Mosque, App dynamic themes etc

Top Reciters

Abdul Rahman Al-Sudais

Abdul Rahman Ibn Abdul Aziz al-Sudais, better known as Al-Sudais, is the imam of the Grand Mosque Masjid al-Haram in Makkah, Saudi Arabia

Saud Al-Shuraim

Saud ibn Ibrahim ibn Muhammad al-Shuraim, is one of the prayer leaders and Friday preachers at the Grand Mosque Masjid al-Haram in Makkah

Mishary bin Rashid A.

Mishari bin Rashed Alafasy, born in Kuwait, is a qāriʾ, imam, preacher, and Nasheed artist. He studied in the Islamic University of Madinah's College of Qur'an

Abdul Basit Samad

Abdul-Basit ‘Abdus-Samad, or Abdel Basit Abdel Samad or, Abdul Basit Muhammad Abdus Samad was an Egyptian Quran reciter and is regarded as one of the best reciters of the Quran who ever lived

Saad El Ghamidi

Saad Al Ghamdi is a Qāriʾ and an imam of the great holy mosque Masjid al-Haram. Al-Ghamdi has served as imam to Muslim communities across the globe, and is currently the imam of Jami Mosque annur in Bekasi

Mohamed Mahmoud Tablawi

Mohamed Mahmoud Tablawi is an Egyptian Imam and Qur’an reciter. He was born in 1934 in a village called Met Oqba, Egypt
